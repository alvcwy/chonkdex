Dug

Height 60 cm
Weight 40 kg

From the Pixar movie 'Up', Dug has a special collar that gives him the ability to talk to humans. Dug is a playful, optimistic, friendly, and lovable dog who is always kind to those he loves. He likes almost everybody he comes across. He is also very good at following rules and can be somewhat of an airhead at times. Dug also gets easily distracted by some things, mainly squirrels. He is a very social dog.
